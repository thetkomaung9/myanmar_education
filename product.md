## Project Overview

EduSphere Myanmar is a School Management & E-Class Platform designed to
digitize administrative and academic processes for schools in Myanmar.

## Vision

> Transforming Myanmar's education sector through technology.

## Mission

-   Digitize school management processes.
-   Reduce administrative workload for teachers.
-   Create an E-Learning environment for students.
-   Improve communication between schools and parents.
